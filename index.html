Kimahri

Here's Kimahri or "Mari" as we like to call him. He's a Pekingese and big brother to Lunafreya. He is fun, gentle and often described as derpy. Although Kimahri loves being fussed he still loves his own personal space when he gets sleepy. He enjoys playing with toys, empty plastic bottles but most of all playing with his little sister Lunafreya.

Lunafreya

Here's Lunafreya. She goes by many names including: Luna, Moon, Pig, Little Piggle, Little Pilg and Moon Pyuuuug. She's an adorable and smaller Pekingese compared to her brother. Although smaller she still has a massive personality. She loves snuggles, beating up her brother, grunting when she wants something and eating all the blueberries. Sadly Lunafreya has a medical condition known as a Liver shunt which means she can't eat normal foods like her brother, however this doesn't stop her from enjoying the best things in life.

Vaan

He's smart, funny and always ready for action. Vaan gets on well with people he meets, he likes to share his things and give lots of kisses at bedtime. He wants to be a firefighter when he's older which is very brave.

Tidus

Oh and he likes frogs.
